## 🌏 Global + emerging market opportunities

::: warning 💡 Why creators outside US/EU have advantages

**1. Cost gap** — Cloud + tools $50-200/month vs. local salary $800-3K/month → can afford full stack while US devs ($5-15K/month salary) face same tool cost.

**2. Underserved niches** —
- Local-language portrait / wedding photo AI
- Regional music AI (bolero in Vietnam, sertanejo in Brazil, K-indie in Korea)
- KOL ảo for local brands (Viettel did Vi An — 100 other VN brands haven't)
- Visa/passport photo AI for emigrating users

**3. Strong communities** —
- **SDVN** (Stable Diffusion Vietnam) ~1,000 active
- Brazil AI creator scene
- Indonesia, Philippines TikTok creator communities
- Korean AI K-pop production
:::

| **Sep 2025** | Spotify bans real-person voice cloning | Don't clone celebrity voices |
