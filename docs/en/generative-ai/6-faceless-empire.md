## 08 Common pitfalls

::: warning 🚨 7 mistakes

**1. Generic AI voice** → audience detects = low trust. Use custom voice + emotion tags

**2. Lazy thumbnail** → low CTR, video doesn't reach. Invest in A/B testing thumbnails

**3. Too many niches at once** → 1 channel = 1 niche. Multi-niche = burnout + algo confused

**4. No format A/B testing** → stuck on 1 format. Try 1 variation each week

**5. Skip SEO** → upload but forget title/tag optimization. Use TubeBuddy / VidIQ

**6. Volume > Quality** → 10 shitty videos/day = brand damage. 3 quality videos/week better

**7. Forget monetization plan** → accumulate 100K views without setting up AdSense / affiliate / shop
:::
