# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

**Learning AI** (operated by AIECOS) is a **free, open, static educational website**. We collect minimal data and respect your privacy.

## What we collect

- **No accounts, no login:** you read the content without providing personal information.
- **Anonymous technical data:** hosting platforms (GitHub Pages, Vercel) may keep standard access logs (IP, browser) for operation and security.
- **Anonymous analytics (if any):** page-view stats to improve content, not personally identifying.
- **Local storage:** only to remember interface settings (light/dark mode, font size) in your browser — never sent to a server.

## What we do NOT do

- We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
- We do not run behavioral tracking ads.
- We do not request sensitive information.

## Third-party links & tools

The content links to external services (GitHub, YouTube, ChatGPT, AI tools…). When you visit them, **their privacy policies** apply — please read them on the respective sites.

## Privacy contact

Questions or requests about your data? Email **[learning@aiecos.ai](mailto:learning@aiecos.ai)** or see the [Contact](./contact) page.
