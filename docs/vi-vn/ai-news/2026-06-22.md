#### Cap nhat San pham & Cong cu
* **OpenAI x Samsung Electronics:** OpenAI cong bo ngay `21/06/2026` viec Samsung Electronics dua `ChatGPT Enterprise` va `Codex` vao su dung cho toan bo nhan vien Samsung tai Han Quoc va nhan vien `Device eXperience (DX)` tren toan cau. Day la mot trong nhung dot trien khai enterprise lon nhat cua OpenAI den thoi diem cong bo, cho thay `Codex` dang duoc dinh vi khong chi cho lap trinh ma con cho workflow tri thuc va van hanh noi bo. Diem quan trong voi doi ngu cong nghe la scale enterprise nay di kem yeu cau governance that su: OpenAI nhan manh cac lop `data protection`, `user and access management`, va `security controls`, con Samsung du kien dung AI cho R&D, manufacturing, marketing, product development, va document work. OpenAI dong thoi cho biet hon `5 million` nguoi dang dung Codex moi tuan, va weekly active users cua Codex tai Han Quoc da tang gan `800%` tu `01/02/2026`. Nguon: [OpenAI - Samsung Electronics brings ChatGPT and Codex to employees](https://openai.com/index/samsung-electronics-chatgpt-codex-deployment/).
